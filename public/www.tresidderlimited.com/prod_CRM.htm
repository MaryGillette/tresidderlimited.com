Microsoft Customer Relationship Management

Microsoft CRM helps businesses build profitable customer relationships. Microsoft CRM offers integrated Sales and Customer Service modules that let employees easily share information across the organization and enable businesses to implement consistent and automated processes. Easy to use, customize, and set up, Microsoft CRM is accessible from both Microsoft® Outlook® and the Web, as well as offers a flexible, scalable Microsoft .NET architecture that integrates easily with Microsoft Office, Microsoft Business Solutions Financials, and third-party applications.


• Improve Business Productivity
Microsoft CRM is designed from the ground up to help you sell more effectively, provide better service to your customers, and give you deeper knowledge of customers and sales activity. You'll make informed business decisions faster.

• Keep Total Ownership Costs Low
With little consulting, short setup time, and minimal training, Microsoft CRM is easy to implement. It's easy to use, and can be customized to fit your specific needs. And, it's ready to grow with your business.

• Integrate Data with Other Business Applications
Microsoft CRM is designed to integrate with other financial and business-management applications to give you a more complete view of customers and your interaction with them.
