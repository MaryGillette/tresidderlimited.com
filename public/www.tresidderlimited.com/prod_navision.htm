Microsoft Business Solutions Navision

More Powerful Analytical Tools

• Microsoft Business Solutions–Navision 4.0 displays data about business performance the way you want it with Online Analytical Processing (OLAP)
• Easy analysis with an advanced Microsoft Windows viewer, a report writer, a Web interface, and capabilities for data mining
• New features for reporting, Analysis Reports, Sales and Purchase Budgets, and Analysis views by Dimensions (ideal for smaller companies with few concurrent users and data of limited complexity)
• New key performance indicators (KPIs) for financial management, supply chain management, and CRM can be viewed in a generic graph component, and also allow you to drill down into your Microsoft Navision application for even better business insight

Better Control of Your Financial Information

• Detailed visibility into every transaction
• Inter-company transactions between any two companies within a group controlled by the same legal entity
• Transparency and analysis tools for efficient processing of sales orders
• Design your own simple reports for better analysis
• Improved user interface on sales documents as well as new features for employees who require simple reporting functionality

Partial Payment and Consolidation Modifications

• Reverse all postings and changes related to an application, regarding both customer ledger entries and vendor ledger entries
• Reverse entries created from lines manually entered in the general ledger, including a clear audit trail of the process
• Global customers can choose which method to use when translating the general ledger account from a foreign entity

Personalization and Navigation Features

• Easier navigation helps employees work more efficiently
• Employees can personalize their own menus
• Manufacturing Foundation helps the production planner manage his or her work

Notifications and Alerts

• Prevent, rather than react to, potential show-stoppers in your company processes
• Generate automatic e-mail messages to your employees, suppliers, partners, and customers to inform them of business-critical issues

Built to Adapt

• Improved upgrade toolkit makes it possible for partners to reduce upgrade costs
• Expertise of independent software vendors makes it easier to implement a business-specific solution that suits your budget
• Retain the flexibility to respond to, add more capacity, and adapt to changing market conditions
• Simplified foundation available for manufacturers that rely on manual planning and straightforward production processes, with option to add modules as manufacturing processes become more complex

Better Together with Microsoft Office

• New main menu navigation pane, similar to the one in Microsoft Office Outlook 2003, can be tailored to your specific needs
