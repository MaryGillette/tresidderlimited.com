Welcome

Tresidder Limited offers consulting services to clients in all five boroughs of New York, and throughout the Tri-State area.
A Microsoft Certified Partner, Tresidder Limited specializes in the Microsoft Business Solutions product family, including Microsoft Dynamics GP (formerly Great Plains), Small Business Financials and FRx Analytics.

Whether your Accounting department consists of 2 employees or 200, our Sales team will work closely with you to find the right software and hardware to improve your workflow, increase your productivity, and ensure compliance with GAAP and, if necessary, Sarbanes-Oxley. Afterwards, our consultants will work tirelessly to provide a seamless transition from your current accounting software to the MBS platform. This includes Network Topology analysis, migration services, SQL Server implementation, 1-on-1 software familiarization classes, and Backup / Disaster Recovery training. Once you've made the transition to Dynamics, we will continue to provide RealTime support to help you get the most out of your software.
