Microsoft Business Solutions FRx Forecaster

FRx ® Forecaster makes it easy for your customers to immediately realize the benefits of budgeting and planning. It includes great new features and enhancements that make building and tracking budgets easier and more efficient than ever before.

By integrating FRx ® Financial Reporter, FRx Forecaster and either Dynamics, Solomon or eEnterprise, we offer advanced financial planning, reporting, and analytic capabilities that allow your customers to take immediate control of their financial reporting and budgeting processes.

FRx ® ExpressLink

This new application saves time and ensures the accuracy of your baseline budget by populating the FRx Forecaster database directly from your general ledger.

• Easily map your GL accounts and balances to FRx Forecaster.

• Consolidate information from the GL before populating budget fields.

• Perform periodic updates of segments and balances.

 

FRx ® DirectLink Interface

Preparing reports that compare actual and budget data is a snap because of the tight integration between your general ledger and FRx Forecaster via FRx Financial Reporter.

• Access budget data directly without moving it back into the GL.

• Map FRx Forecaster to the GL for each unit of the Reporting Tree.

• Choose from 12 output options; including OLAP, XML, Excel, etc.

 

Input Screens

You can now define a flexible input screen with up to 100 different periods! Columns can be defined to calculate information that can serve as a guide for a new plan. New screens have also been added . The Single Account Input Screen makes it easy to increase the baseline budget by a percentage or amount. A notes page can display an account history or a note about the account. Budgets now store up to 13 periods to support additional views.

 

Human Resources

New HR functionality makes it easier to budget for employees in complex scenarios. Detailed compensation records are stored in the database, which makes it convenient to pull information into account-level views.

• Budget employees across cost centers with all tax and benefit information enforced.

• Be more flexible in assigning salaries, salary increases, and bonuses.

• Budget employees on a monthly or annual basis.

• Budget employees as hourly or salaried, and toggle between them.

• Track employees by defining both hours and FTE/headcount.

 

Drill Back to FRx Forecaster Detail

New functionality now allows an FRx Financial Reporter report to combine FRx Forecaster and GL data into the same report. Then, when drilling down on this info, the FRx ® DrillDown Viewer™ will allow you to drill back into the appropriate source.

 

Benefits

The Benefits screen has been improved to provide five user-defined fields to qualify individual employees for benefits.

• Define an incremented benefits tier to apply fixed benefits—flat rate, fixed amount, or fixed amount incremented.

• Define and enforce limits on individual benefits.

 

Views

New views use a breakout feature to show detailed information for each account.

• Display 100 columns in a single view.

• Create views that display and drill down into compensation information.
