eOne

eOne has developed a number of extension products adding value to the core Microsoft Great Plains application.

  • eXtender
    eXtender adds a level of flexibility to Great Plains and Small Business Financials that has not previously been available. eXtender enables end users to make fast, easy modifications and customizations to their solution or create brand new applications to meet specific requirements.
  • Office Reports
    Create reports that print directly to Excel, Word and Outlook from inside Great Plains.
  • SmartList Builder
    Create you own SmartLists, even from third party products. Available for Great Plains and Small Business Manager.
  • Flexicoder
    This powerful tool is ideal for all distribution customers that have a complex general ledger. Flexicoder brings the power of tier one ERP solutions to Great Plains customers. Code your General Ledger the way you need it, for the best departmental reporting.
