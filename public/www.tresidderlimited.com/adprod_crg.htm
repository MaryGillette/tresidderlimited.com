Corporate Renaissance Group

CRG offers innovative solutions that drive better business management for Microsoft Business Solutions Great Plains, Small Business Financials, and Navision customers.

With a suite of Data Automation, Employee Performance Management, and Financial Planning and Analysis tools, thousands of customers worldwide have been able to make better decisions and increase their productivity.

CRG Add-ons for MBS Products:

  • Changer
    Effortlessly change and merge GL Accounts, Item numbers, Vendor ID's, and Customer Numbers for all current and historical transactions. Changer also transfers Employee Numbers in Great Plains for all transactions to new employee numbers, including Project Accounting and Fixed Assets.
  • Re-Formatter
    Re-Formatter seamlessly reformats the Chart of Accounts and Account Framework, instantaneously altering the format of all data, including maximum account length, size of segments, number of segments, and sorting options. With Re-Formatter, all historical data is modified as the old account format is mapped to the new account format.
  • Reverser
    Reverser is a dynamic and intelligent business solution that efficiently reverses, rectifies, or re-posts journal entries when the need arises. Reverser can save hundreds of data-entry hours, as transactions can effortlessly be corrected without having to manually re-enter any information.
  • Merger Series
    Merger Series allows you to merge orders, invoices, or batches to streamline your sales order processing. Seamlessly integrated with Microsoft Business Solutions Great Plains Sales Order Processing (SOP), the Merger Series generates productivity gains, while maintaining the highest level of data integrity. This feature allows businesses to maximize the use of data stored in their Great Plains system to improve productivity and increase bottom line results.
  • emPerform
    emPerform is a web-based employee performance management solution that helps organizations move away from traditional paper-based systems. By incorporating an online employee performance management solution, successful organizations are able to help people improve and succeed. Each employee can set and track their current goals, while staying focused on developing the right set of skills and competencies for the future.
  • mynSight
         Save time getting to the information you need. Use current reporting tools that you are comfortable with. Leverage existing data in Great Plains that you did not know could be accessed. Examine the data from multiple budgets, companies and MDA codes, as well as non-Great Plains data. CRG invested its world-renowned BI expertise to produce a solution that will transform your data into knowledge, knowledge into action, and action into success.
  • Cost Allocator
    Cost Allocator is a simple cost management tool that helps Financial Controllers understand full costs.  It matches expenditures more closely with where they originate - giving organizations the ability to accurately determine full costs, enhance accountability and improve their decision making.
  • Budget Maestro
         A complete budgeting, planning, and management software solution; Budget Maestro automates the process of budgeting, planning, forecasting, and consolidation. With Budget Maestro's unique multi-dimensional technology you will now be able to go beyond the limitations of spreadsheets and create multiple scenarios across all dimensions of your business.
